Who We Are



Founded by Greenestep Technologies, we are a growing start-up company that specializes in providing essential business services. With our founders having held executive positions at reputable companies such as Everest Software Inc, Greenestep, and Console Connect, we've seen the entirety of several industries such as ecommerce, saas, services, and non-profits. Big companies have always been exposed to the luxury of having no problems with the providing of business services, infrastructure, and back office support but what about small to medium-sized companies?


As former small business owners, we know that time spent dealing with accounting, invoicing, payroll, auditing, inventory management, and tax compliance is a great deal of time and effort that could be utilized for building up your core business. Additionally, there's always been the pain of trying to outsource the back office without having to deal with issues such as managing multiple vendors.


With this, we decided to make a change and develop solutions that help assist in fundamental business processes. We've developed flexible and customized solutions to ensure that your back office runs efficiently. Affirms provides you with a support team that your company can turn to for any questions, devising financial strategies, and for being able to see your business progression. We are Affirms, your one solution for everything.


Who We Serve


Our target customers that we hope to serve are small to mid-sized companies. We hope to help them grow and thrive and help them push boundaries, not paperwork. We guarantee our clients the following suite of benefits:

  • 1. Access to high quality service and resources
  • 2. Faster turnaround time
  • 3. Increased profits
  • 4. Cost cutting solution

Our outsourcing business services include outsourced accounting, internal audit and cpa service, HR and payroll management, tax preparation and planning, controller and cfo service.
