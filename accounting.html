Accounting & Inventory Done at Affirms



Financially, at Affirms we've got you covered. We take pride in making data-driven decisions and providing you full-scale services ranging from inventory management to compliant accounting and budgeting. Affirms will help you be trusted by your stakeholders as well as accelerate your funding/sales to achieve your financial goals.



By outsourcing your accounting to us, we guarantee these three core benefits:


Core Company Growth

Your team gets to focus more on the growth of the core company and all integral processes by outsourcing your accounting to us.

Flexible Teams

In your workflow environment, our team can be scaled and grow based on the priority and tasks.

Dedicated Full-Time Staff

Our agents work for nothing but your success and hope to continue growing your business.


Below are some of the features you get with our outsourced accounting and why you should choose our company.


Up-to-date financials

We manage your accounts payable entries and ensure no invoice goes unattended. Additionally, we continuously stay on top of your payments to supplies such that any money owed to you continues to flow into your account.

Audit-Proof Records

When it comes to dealing with possible audits, risk is cut down significantly with our expertise accounting services that arise. We keep your records in line with all regulations to ensure complete compliance.


Unlimited Accessibility to Bookkeeping

24/7 access anytime anywhere to all accounting records. Our support team will also be able to take you through all records and clear out any concerns or questions that you have. We will also help you perform analyses on dashboards and reports to make smarter financial decisions.

No Financial Fraud

Our team regularly reviews all credit card and bank statements to confirm that the transactions make sense when cross-checking with balance sheets, invoices, and receipts. If anything doesn't add up, our team will immediately go into investigation and ensure that any problems are resolved so you don't have to face the case of fraud.


An Essential Financial Voice

We will have a financial head from our end that will help not only communicate with you but help monitor employee productivity to ensure quality and efficient work. This head will also participate in any of your executive meetings to provide you insight/updates on the status of financial goals and how company growth can be advanced further.

Optimized Processes & Growth

Our team takes pride in being able to fully utilize KPIs to further advance the growth of your business. Additionally, we will provide you with our CRM software so that communication from both ends will be more efficient. Our integrated software allows for you to be able to access accurate data and gain insight on the health of your business.

Forecasting and Planning

Our team provides you with financial forecasting and planning insights to determine how business decisions will affect the short-term and long-term growth of your company.

Financial Analysis

During our analysis, our team tries to find patterns and trends with all data financially and determine which parts are both helping your business growth as well as hindering it. This is scaled up to the CFO level when it comes to all financial tools.

Cash Application and Planning

Our accounting team monitors your company’s cash inflow as well as help you organize all financial records. Our team considers it a priority to provide your company with regular reports for all financial records.

Settling Debts

After gathering details about the company that you owe money to, we make sure debt collectors are aware. Once we contact the company, we find the reason behind any form of debt or whether anything has been unpaid. Using this reason, our team comes up with a strategy to make sure not only the company is paid but to help ensure that incidents like this don't happen in the future.

GL Management

When reviewing over key accounts and implementing all recommended changes, we create an accurate account of your general ledgers. Our team reviews and analyzes the general ledger trial balance to make sure that there are any abnormal and inappropriate balances. Our experts develop controls to ensure the integrity of financial statement data.

Budget Report

We provide a report for how company’s spending vs. revenue and whether it meets the financial forecasting projections to identify areas over or under budget. A monthly report is prepared and reviewed with financial statements to determine areas of the business that are not meeting expectations.

Software Integrations

We can work with your current accounting software or provide you with our accounting software to help kickstart your business. With your current software, our team can integrate them so all critical information from across your business is easily accessible.


By outsourcing your inventory to us, we guarantee these four core benefits:


Inventory Essentials

With our inventory system, you get detailed visibility into key inventory control and supply chain management. This includes inventory/stock trends and supplier performance.

Automated Purchasing

Affirms makes the switch to lower cost and higher control automated purchasing.

Cost-Effective System

With one system in place, IT costs are eliminated and any concerns that are associated with maintaining and upgrading applications.

Order Fulfillment

All data entry, organization, and fulfillment are done through our inventory system. You can immediately convert leads to orders, orders to shipments, and shipments to revenue in short order.


Below are some of the specific features and functionalities that we would have in our inventory system:


Dashboard Features:

  • Track pricing, minimum order quantities, and production lead time
  • Automatically generate purchase orders when stock is low
  • Multi-channel landscape that displays all channels, warehouses, and products shown
  • Can identify patterns and opportunities in your business for growth
  • Instant adding of multiple in-house warehouses and 3PLs with automatic shipping and return labels
  • Displays turnover, gross percent of margin, customer fulfillment rate, carrying costs, inventory age, and locked inventory
  • View inventory values on a per SKU
  • Rapid and accurate response to new orders

Tasks that inventory system can carry out:

  • Spit fulfillment of orders based on inventory available across warehouse or product labels
  • Tracks inventory at different stock locations across your warehouse
  • Automates repetitive/time-intensive tasks such as assigning weights/dimensions, package types, and calculating reorder quantities
  • Split bundled products within an order into its individual core components
  • Calculates maximum quantity possible for each bundle and kit using core products and pre-packaged inventory
  • Cross-channel reporting with detailed data on revenue, order, and units sold
  • Maintaining control as sales volume increases across multiple channels
  • Creates and ranks multiple pick locations per SKU per warehouse to receive POs and fulfill orders
  • Manages multiple FNSKUs for one master SKU
