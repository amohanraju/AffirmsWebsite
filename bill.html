Billing Services


Billing Done at Affirms


At Affirms, you get nothing but the best billing services from us. We prioritize making all invoicing and payment processes as fast and efficient as possible so you can focus more on your core business.

Below are some benefits that you get by outsourcing your billing to us:



Unlimited Online Document Storage


Can easily access all history of past invoices, proof of payment, and other important documents.

Flexibility with Bill Management


Be able to manage your bill pay anywhere. You get to pay vendors how they would like to be paid. This can be any forms of payments such as ACH, check, international wire transfer, and vendor direct virtual cards.

Automatic Integration



All of the data entered in our accounting system is automatically synced with our billing software so no need to enter data in twice which saves your time.


Automated Processes


No need to enter data in manually. With our billing system, we automate it all for you including data entry, tedious paperwork, and approval chasing.

Fax Billing Service


We provide this as an option for you in order to eliminate having to install a separate on-premise server. You are able to pay for only what you use and scale it to your needs.

Streamlined Processes


Your invoices are processed and streamlined easily. This allows us to speed up your payment approval process.
