HR & Payroll Services


HR & Payroll Done at Affirms


Affirms provides your company with an all-in one platform. Our services provide you with a range of benefits such as management of employee benefits, ensuring staff is paid on time, and providing the tools and training needed for the growth of your company. We provide you with HR reports that keep an effective record of any business updates.



HR Done



Here at Affirms, our HR consulting team takes absolute priority in not only helping build good relationships amongst current employees but we hope to make sure that new employees feel welcome and can adjust into their new roles and duties.


To help ensure this, there are several things our HR team does in the process:


Employee Handbook

Working with your staff, we create an employee handbook that all employees can reference for HR policies.

Onboarding

We create your onboarding checklist and compliance system for you to ensure that onboarding at your company helps new employees feel welcomed.

Offboarding

Our team makes sure that everything in the offboarding process such as forms, exit interviews, and other responsibilities are taken care of.

Compliance

We help set up a compliance system that streamlines the entire process of getting all staff to get to know about company policies and procedures.

Background Checks

During the process of hiring, background checks are extremely essential when getting to know about prospects. We provide resources to help you make your background check process more efficient.

Employee Relations

Our team works with your team to ensure good relationships between staff and the company.

Performance Management

Our team does extensive research when it come to compensation with employees to help you make the right decisions regarding how much to pay employees based on their performance. We provide performance management to ensure that all employees are putting in their best efforts. Through following our advised benefits that should be provided to employees such as insurance, flexible spending accounts, and paid leave, we hope to help you build a strong, productive workforce.

Employee Benefits Assistance

Our team can help to answer questions for your employees about any sort of benefits they would receive. Additionally, we continuously work to keep employees updated about benefits changes and open enrollment periods.

Carrier Liaison

Our team with our solutions help you give the best coverage for your staff. We coordinate with employee benefits providers and oversee all insurance aspects.



Recruiting Done



As part of our HR, we provide your company with recruitment support services to help you pick out the best employees for your company.


Affirms works with your staff and utilizes proprietary sourcing to find the right candidates. Our experienced team of recruiters will do everything that is required for screening applicants such as drafting job descriptions, getting candidates, conducting screenings, scheduling job interviews, and organizing everything for travel. From there after all interviews are conducted, we work with the company managers to assess candidates and help make final decisions.



Payroll Done



With our payroll services, we ensure that all payments are the correct amount and deposited on time. Through our automation, we hope to increase accuracy with this as well as mimimize the time that is spent getting paychecks to employees.


Our payroll management takes care of everything related to time reporting, taxes/deductions, and direct deposits.


Additionally, our team can integrate payroll with your accounting software or the accounting software you are using from us such that all payroll expenses are kept track of.




Have any questions about our services? We can help address your concerns and answer any questions!
