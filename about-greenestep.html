Who We Are

Founded in 2008, GreeneStep is a private company, with offices all over the world including locations in India, US and Singapore. They develop business management and collaboration software(ERP, CRMx, Inventory, Orders Processing, Accounting, Webstores and B2B Portals) as an integrated solution for distribution, discrete manufacturing and Ecommerce companies to manage their operations, streamline supply chain and accounting.


About Our Company



Greenestep currently services over 120+ companies and clients across US, Canada, India, UK, Singapore and Middle East. With our customer success and reputation, recently CIO Review named Greenestep as one of the "20 most promising ERP solution providers of 2018". Recently, we have partnered with a company, ConverSight, to be able to apply conversational AI towards our products such that along with advanced platforms and flexibility with scaling/meeting goals, business owners have the utmost convenience towards accessing their entire business anytime and anywhere.

Mission: Help small to mid-sized business owners grow and succeed. With our greenestep solutions and supportive network/team, we hope to help your company generate good wealth, health, and peace of mind for all employees, associates, customer's employees and stakeholders
