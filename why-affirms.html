Why Choose Affirms?


Why Us



Here at Affirms, we take pride in providing you with both software as well as a support team that helps you find the top talent, helps optimize business processes, and provides you with the essential financial tools you need for success. When deciding which company is the best option for you, we break it down into five different factors that you would have to look for. We explain how we exceed your expectations for these factors below.

Pricing

When it comes to business suite software, installation, business process outsourcing, or back office support we make sure that you don't have to make big up-front investments but manage both software and services for a monthly subscription. We charge a flat rate for collaboration tools, CRM, support services, including back office support and outsourced accounting. We are flexible and scale our operational support based on company growth, and increased number of employees. Discuss with us today to get a customized quote for you!


Our Experience

For over 25 years, our staff has been in the IT, finance, accounting, and tax industry so we guarantee only the best support and products. We prioritize proving services that are up to date and convenient for our customers. Our back office and IT support is available at all times filled with only the best people for your business.


Product Flexibility & Communication

All our services including software services, tech support, and business process outsourcing(BPO) are all provided remotely through the cloud. Additionally, we provide you with collaboration tools to help communicate effectively with our team. We offer a wide range of products and services so based on your business needs, we can help find the perfect business solution/package for your company.


Quality of work

When you outsource your business to us, we ensure that software service or outsourced service are managed efficiently, while you get to focus on more important aspects of your core business. To ensure quality of work, from our end we hire professionals to work with you and to give you required support regarding the business process, helping to give a run-down on updates during meetings, and monitoring employees to make sure productivity is high.


Reputation and financial stability

Being backed by Greenestep Technologies, we guarantee that we're financially stable and among some of the only startups to not fall under in the BPO industry. Most companies that are in the BPO industry have little to no experience, but this is not the case with us. With our experience staff and support team, we know how to be stable and excel in the industry and market.
