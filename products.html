Affirms For You


At Affirms, there are two package options you could go for in order to help your business. These options include: Business Suite Software Package and the Outsourcing Services Package. On top of being able to purchase these individually, customers have the flexibility of being able to bundle packages together and purchase services with software. We have provided details regarding the different packages below. To discuss further details/pricing of the different packages, contact us and schedule your free consultation today!





All-In One Business Suite Software




This business suite software, provided by Greenestep, is built on cutting edge MS technology and is both configurable as well as easily scalable.


The suite software consists of an ERP Business Solution along with CRM and Collaboration System. This back office ERP is a cloud-based solution that integrates together all your people, departments, and customers. The ERP software provides you with key details and information about various business areas such as Inventory of Product and Services, CRM and Prospect Management, Sales Orders and Invoicing, Retail Point of Sales, Purchases and Suppliers Management, Warehouse Management and Shipping, Production and Job Orders Management, Financial Management, and Accounting.


As part of CRMx collaboration system, we help facilitate remote working for your employees from any location on any smart device. Some of the features provided by this collaboration system include an employee portal, customers portal, supplier/vendor portal, project tracking, field servicing and billing, and production tracking. Additionally, various dashboards are provided including performance dashboards that summarize performance in various departments/operations as well as user specific dashboards.
